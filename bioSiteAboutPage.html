About Billy

William Hendrix Crandall was born in 1961 in Birmingham, AL. He has lived in various cities throughout the Birmingham metro area, but for the past 24 years, has called Pelham home. He can be described as hard working, driven, resourceful, helpful, fair, goofy, and happy as a clam. He has worked in many different fields throughout his career, starting as a bag boy at Macks Quick Sack in Vestavia when he turned 15. Since then, he has worked as a stable hand for the trail horses at Oak Mountain State Park, a NICU life-line nurse, a pharmaceutical sales representative, the Director of Parks and Recreation for Pelham, and after realizing the retired life was not for him, decided to go back into healthcare as a nurse in the local hospital's psychiatric division.
